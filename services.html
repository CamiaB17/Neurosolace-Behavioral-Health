What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive behavioral health care for children (ages 7+) and adults via telehealth.

Medication Management

Thorough psychiatric evaluation and evidence-based prescribing of medications for a wide range of conditions. Includes ongoing monitoring, dosage adjustments, and coordination with your other healthcare providers.

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation
  • Medication initiation and monitoring
  • Dosage reviews and adjustments
  • Management of anxiety, depression, ADHD, bipolar disorder, and more

Psychiatric Consultations & Second Opinions

For patients who want additional expert input on their diagnosis, current treatment plan, or medications. We provide thorough, unbiased assessments to help you feel confident in your mental health journey.

  • Comprehensive diagnostic review
  • Treatment plan evaluation
  • Medication second opinions
  • Coordination with existing providers

Therapy

Supportive therapy addressing stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, relationship issues, and more. Sessions are tailored to each patient's unique background, needs, and goals — in a safe, non-judgmental environment.

  • Individual therapy for children (7+) and adults
  • Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Telehealth options available

Conditions We Treat

Anxiety / GAD Depression ADHD Bipolar Disorder PTSD OCD Schizophrenia Eating Disorders Social Anxiety Personality Disorders Trauma & Grief And more...
